About BTS

BTS, which is also known as Bangtan Boys is a South Korean Boy Band grouped in 2013. The group comprises seven talented members: RM (Kim Namjoon), the charismatic leader; Jin (Kim Seokjin), the eldest with a heartwarming voice; Suga (Min Yoongi), a genius producer and rapper; J-Hope (Jung Hoseok), the sunshine of the group known for his dance and rap skills; Jimin (Park Jimin), the graceful dancer with a mesmerizing voice; V (Kim Taehyung), the unique and soulful vocalist;and Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook), the youngest or "Golden Maknae" who excels in everything he does. Each of them has unique talents.They have won billions of heart by their music.Their fandom name is "ARMY" which is also named as "Most Loyal Fandom". They not only famous for music but also for their way of connecting with their fans. They have won more than 500 awards with their talents and charm.Kim Namjoon is the Leader of BTS.
